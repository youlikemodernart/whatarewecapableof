01 · What Kamp Songs Is

The worship DNA of the ministry.

Kamp Songs writes, records, and releases original Kamp Love worship songs. Retreats train worship team leads from campuses across the country, then the songs are recorded and sent out to serve churches and camps.

It is a discipleship pipeline for young worship leaders. Music is the byproduct of formation.

02 · The Rhythm

Write. Record. Release.

01

Write on retreat.

Songs are co-written by worship leaders from Kamp Love campuses and tested in the room.

02

Record live.

One room, one take, one congregation. The sound carries the imprint of the moment.

03

Release for the Church.

Songs go out on major platforms for churches, student ministries, and worship sets.

04

Train leaders.

Every song is attached to formation, not only distribution.

03 · The Vision

One thousand young worship leaders.

Equipped to write and release original worship music for the Church. Formed on retreats. Sent into their local contexts.
