The Board · 2026

The men and women who carry the mission with us.

Kamp Love is built by a body. The board provides governance, counsel, and covering for the work ahead.

01 · Why The Board Matters

Counsel, covering, and accountability.

Kamp Love is a vehicle. The mission is the Lord's. The board exists so the founder, the staff, and the body of work are stewarded faithfully: spiritually, legally, financially, and strategically.

02 · The Board

Six leaders. One mission.

Kent Wisenor

Vice President of Sales · Bay Area, California

Kent leads enterprise sales for a cloud-native infrastructure company in the San Francisco Bay Area. With more than two decades building and coaching high-performing go-to-market teams, he brings clarity, accountability, and execution to Kamp Love.

Noah Wolfe

Sr. Director of Development, GCU · Phoenix, Arizona

Noah serves as Senior Director of Development at Grand Canyon University, helping people invest in mission-driven Christian higher education. He brings relational fluency, fundraising discipline, and a heart for college-aged disciples.

Edy Sutherland

Disciple-Maker · Faith in the Outdoors

Edy has built ministry at the intersection of faith and the outdoors. Her work mirrors the Kamp Love conviction that students are transformed when they step off campus and into God's creation.

Skip Powell

Emergency Medicine Physician · Real Estate Entrepreneur

Skip practices emergency medicine and has built a real estate investment portfolio that supports his next chapter of giving. Earlier seasons serving with Christian camping continue to shape how he reads the Kamp Love mission.

Tim Rockwell

Business Owner & Growth Consultant · Madison, Wisconsin

Tim is a business owner and growth consultant in Madison, Wisconsin. Across more than three decades in business, he has founded, led, and advised companies through important inflection points.

Peter Swenson

Serial Entrepreneur · Founder & CEO of HIRE

Peter is a serial entrepreneur and the Founder and CEO of HIRE, a Phoenix-based recruiting firm. He brings entrepreneurial instinct, recruiting expertise, and field-sales team-building experience to the board.

03 · Board Structure

Four boards. One mission.

Kamp Love is governed by complementary boards that steward the legal, strategic, spiritual, and personal dimensions of the work.

Legal

Fiduciary and legal oversight: compliance, governance, and protection.

Advisory

Strategic counsel from proven operators and ministry leaders.

Pastoral

Spiritual covering for staff, founder, and organizational health.

Founder's Advisors

Personal counsel to the founder on vision, family, and long-term stewardship.
