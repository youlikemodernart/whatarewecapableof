Text equivalent

The MVP portal has Course home at /sales-school. Course home contains Today, Day One, Day Two, Resources, and Facilitator. Today is the live-session surface. Day One contains Breakouts 1, 2, and 3. Day Two contains Breakouts 4, 5, and 6. Each breakout page uses media from Wistia. Search, progress, admin editing, cohorts, and login are optional later features rather than MVP pages.

Queries, omissions, and legend

Viewer should be able to

  • Find Course home as the root of the proposed portal.
  • See Today as the live-session surface, not a generic dashboard.
  • See Day One and Day Two as peer course sections.
  • Confirm that six breakout pages sit under the two day sections.
  • See Resources and Facilitator as first-build peer support pages.
  • Confirm that Wistia remains the media layer.
  • Keep optional later features separate from MVP scope.

Omissions

  • payment
  • CRM administration
  • lesson-level page expansion
  • transcript indexing in the visible diagram
  • support triage detail
  • exact login policy
