WAWCO TrendScope

Pinterest trend intelligence for brands and agencies. Discover what's emerging before it peaks.

What it does

TrendScope connects to your Pinterest account and surfaces the search trends, related terms, and category data that signal where visual culture is heading. Built for creative strategists, brand marketers, and agency researchers who need to back creative decisions with platform data.

Features

Search trend tracking. Monitor how search volume shifts across terms and categories over time. Spot rising trends before they hit the mainstream.

Related term discovery. See what adjacent searches Pinterest connects to your terms. Useful for content strategy, keyword planning, and understanding how audiences navigate a topic space.

Audience analytics. View impression, click, save, and outbound click data for your account and content. Understand what's landing and what's not.

Competitive landscape. Map the trend space around your brand. Identify which search territories are crowded and which are open.

Content strategy briefs. Export trend data and insights into strategy briefs for creative teams and content planners.

Who it's for

Brand strategists tracking category trends. Agency researchers building creative briefs. Content marketers planning editorial calendars. Anyone who uses Pinterest data to make creative decisions and needs more than the native analytics dashboard.

How it works

Connect your Pinterest Business account through Pinterest's OAuth consent flow. TrendScope requests read access to your account data and Pinterest's trend and keyword APIs. You control access and can disconnect at any time through your Pinterest account settings.
