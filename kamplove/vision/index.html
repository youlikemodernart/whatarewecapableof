The Vision · 2026

A state of the union. A call for help.

What God has done through Kamp Love, what we are doing now, and the body we are building for the next five years.

Austin Rockwell · Founder

01 · Who We Are

A leader development organization.

Kamp Love does not only expose college students to Christian practices. It trains them to lead those practices for their own generation.

The student who worships at Kamp becomes the worship leader who leads the next Kamp. The student who receives prayer becomes the student who prays over the next one.

02 · Mission · Vision · Model

Three pillars, one direction.

Mission

What we do.

Transform students' lives by the gospel through camping, connection, community, and Christ.

Vision

Where we are going.

Students on every college campus equipped in their gifts, making disciples who make disciples.

Model

How we do it.

Fall trip, spring retreat, and year-round student-led formation on every campus.

Practices

How students lead.

Worship, prayer, testimony, scripture, and baptism, all carried by peers.

03 · My Vision

What I believe God is calling us to build.

  1. Kamp Love at 100 universities in 5 countries, partnered with 100 local churches, by 2030.
  2. Develop 10,000 college student leaders through a six-category discipleship framework.
  3. Create and deliver student-leader development content, opportunity, and coaching.
  4. Own and operate retreat centers across the world.
  5. Build Kamp Love Kids as a curriculum and camp expression for churches.
  6. Build Kamp Love Summer Camp for church campuses and camp locations.
  7. Equip 1,000 young worship leaders through Kamp Songs.

04 · What We Have Done

Five years. Nineteen campuses. Eighteen thousand students.

The cumulative story is momentum: more than 18,000 students reached and 5,550 student leaders developed across 19 campuses over five years.

05 · What We Are Doing

Operating the rhythm.

Fall

Fall trip.

A lower-barrier camping trip where students disconnect from campus noise and enter Christian community.

Spring

Spring retreat.

A deeper weekend that forms worship, prayer, testimony, scripture, and baptism in a peer-led setting.

Year-round

Leader development.

Student leaders are coached on campus all year so the weekend becomes a discipleship pipeline.

Pipeline

The engine.

Student to Student Leader to Student Director to Regional Director. Each tier trains the next.

11 · University-Level Chart

How a Kamp is led on every campus.

Student Directors (3)
Own the Kamp Love community on campus: ministry, operations, and development.
Team Leads (about 10)
Lead worship, prayer, small groups, operations, first impressions, media, finance, food, medical, and other functions.
Student Leaders (50+)
Serve, disciple, invite, and carry the weekend into campus life.
Kampers
Every student we get to reach.
