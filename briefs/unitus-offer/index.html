Scope

A one-time setup that puts the system in place:

  • Subscriber-path check: how new contacts enter and where they go across homepage, events, organic, and paid.
  • First direct build email and SMS batch
  • Light transition benchmark on the new Shopify site when it is ready: capture points, product clarity, CTA hierarchy, mobile read, cart basics, and a prioritized fix list for the builder.

Weekly execution support: $480/week

Ongoing support once setup is done, I am personally targeting around 3 to 4 focused hours a week, but we do what it takes to accomplish the goals!

  • Email and SMS production against the June/July map.
  • Copy and design assembly, build, QA, and scheduling where access is approved.
  • A flagged send when the calendar or data shows one is worth adding.
  • Subscriber-path checks and small fixes.
  • A short weekly status note: what shipped, what is waiting, what is next.

Send volume follows the calendar, the offer, and the data rather than a set quota. Unitus approves anything customer-facing before it goes out.
