Scope

What I will run

Setup

Reference pickup and production target

I pick up the reference garments in the Valley during the LA production pass, translate each piece into a clear production target, and confirm whether the first run stays one-size small or needs a simple size split.

Maker

Oscar first, backups ready

Oscar gets first look as the existing LA maker. I vet his cut-and-sew scope, knit experience, timing, sample process, pricing, and payment terms. If he is not the right fit or timeline, I meet or contact backup LA manufacturers.

Materials

Fabric, trims, and decoration

I source the body fabrics and trims for the ringer tee, tank, and 3/4-sleeve knit. I also coordinate the front-logo path: print, embroidery, or patch, depending on what works best for the garment and timeline.

Inputs

Labels, hang tags, buttons, and artwork

I align the label, hang tag, button, artwork, and trim inputs with the contacts Sophie and Caroline already have, so the maker has what he needs before the sample or bulk cut.

Run

Sampling, production alignment, delivery

The hardest piece gets sampled before the run is awarded. I manage the fit and construction check, pull together the production-cost sheet, track approvals, quality-check against the approved sample, and coordinate delivery.
