Privacy Policy

WAWCO TrendScope

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how What are we capable of? ("WAWCO," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you use WAWCO TrendScope (the "Application"), a Pinterest trend intelligence tool for brands and agencies.

1. Information we collect

The Application accesses the following categories of information from Pinterest, only after you explicitly authorize the connection through Pinterest's OAuth consent screen:

Pinterest account information: Basic profile data including your Pinterest username, user ID, and profile information, as permitted by the scopes you authorize.

Board and pin data: Content from your public and/or secret boards and pins, depending on the permission scopes you grant.

Usage data: We may collect technical information about how the Application is used, such as timestamps of API calls and error logs, for debugging and operational purposes.

2. How we use information

Information accessed through the Pinterest API is used exclusively for the following purposes:

Trend intelligence: Search trend tracking, related term discovery, audience analytics, and content strategy insights delivered through the Application's dashboard.

Application operation: Displaying and organizing Pinterest content within the Application's interface as directed by the authorized user.

Development and improvement: Testing, debugging, and improving the Application's functionality and performance.

We do not use Pinterest data for advertising, user profiling, or any purpose not directly related to the Application's core functionality.

3. Data storage and retention

Access tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored securely using industry-standard practices. Tokens are stored only for the duration necessary to provide the Application's functionality.

Cached content: Pinterest content may be temporarily cached to improve Application performance. Cached data is not shared with third parties.

Retention: You may revoke the Application's access at any time through your Pinterest account settings. Upon revocation, all associated access tokens are invalidated. Cached content is cleared within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your Pinterest data to third parties.

We do not share your Pinterest data with third parties except:

Service providers: We may use infrastructure providers (such as cloud hosting services) to operate the Application. These providers process data only on our instructions and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Legal obligations: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.

5. Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect information accessed through the Application. This includes encrypted storage of credentials, secure transmission of data via HTTPS, and adherence to Pinterest's security requirements for API applications.

In the event of a data breach involving Pinterest data, we will notify affected users and Pinterest in accordance with applicable requirements.

6. Your rights and choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Revoke access: You can disconnect the Application from your Pinterest account at any time through your Pinterest account settings under "Apps."

Data deletion: To request deletion of any Pinterest data stored by the Application, contact us at the email address below. We will respond to deletion requests within 30 days.

Access and portability: You may request a copy of Pinterest data stored by the Application by contacting us.

7. Compliance with Pinterest policies

The Application is developed and operated in compliance with the Pinterest Developer Guidelines, Pinterest API Terms of Service, and Pinterest's data use policies. We adhere to Pinterest's requirements regarding data handling, user consent, and application security.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
